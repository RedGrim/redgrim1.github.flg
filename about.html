About Us

The Frank Lally Golf Classic was established in memory of Frank Lally, a man who dedicated his life to helping others and enriching his community.

This annual event raises funds for the Frank Lally Memorial Scholarship Fund — a scholarship for Roman Catholic high school students who have lost their fathers. The tournament supports scholarships, mentorship programs, and resources to help these students pursue their education and goals.

Event details: Friday, September 26, 2025 — Jeffersonville Golf Club (2400 W. Main Street, Norristown, PA). Shotgun start at 1:00 PM. Trackman simulators open at 11:00 AM; registration opens at 11:30 AM.

All proceeds benefit the Frank Lally Memorial Scholarship Fund. Thank you for your continued support.
