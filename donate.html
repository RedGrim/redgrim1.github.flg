Payments & Donations

This page covers both event payments (player fees, sponsorships) and donations to the Frank Lally Memorial Scholarship Fund. Use the Venmo link below for quick payments or donations. When paying for event items (registration, foursomes, sponsors), please include a note describing the payment (e.g., "Registration — John Smith", "Hole Sponsor — Company Name"). For donations, include "Donation" in the note.
