About Us

[Add Frank Lally's Photo Here]

The Frank Lally Golf Classic was established in memory of Frank Lally, a man who dedicated his life to helping others and enriching his community.

[Add the story behind Frank Lally here - his values, what he meant to the community, and why this charity was started in his honor.]

Our mission is to provide financial support and resources to young boys who have lost their fathers, helping them navigate life's challenges and pursue their dreams. Through this annual golf tournament, we bring together the community to make a meaningful difference in the lives of these young men.

Every dollar raised goes directly to programs and support services that help these boys thrive despite their loss.
